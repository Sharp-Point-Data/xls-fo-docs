Attributes: font
font
The value of this attribute must be the font name of a font
whose embedding is allowed. Licensed fonts may be declared as non-
embeddable. The PDF generator of the AH XSL Formatter strictly
observes this restriction.
Applies to: <embed-font>
Wert: <family-name>
Default: none
